## Project Overview

This project is developed as part of the Digital Literacy course, where I take on the role of a **Student Digital Ambassador**. The goal of this project is to help students understand how to use digital technologies effectively, responsibly, and safely.

A major focus of this project is **cyber security awareness**, ensuring that individuals can recognize online threats and protect their personal and digital information.
## What is Digital Literacy?

Digital literacy is the ability to use digital tools and platforms in a safe, responsible, and effective manner. It involves understanding how to access, evaluate, and share information while being aware of online risks such as phishing, malware, and data theft.

In today’s digital age, digital literacy is essential for maintaining privacy, ensuring security, and making informed decisions online.

## Stay Safe Online

* Never share OTPs or passwords
* Use strong and unique passwords
* Enable two-factor authentication
* Avoid clicking suspicious links
* Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
